# Handai Coffee – Sales & Demand Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes 5,000+ transaction-level sales records (2025) to identify demand patterns, revenue concentration, and operational insights.

The analysis demonstrates an end-to-end workflow including:
- Data cleaning and validation
- Feature engineering
- Exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Business interpretation and recommendations

---

## Key Business Questions
- When does peak demand occur?
- Is there a difference between weekday and weekend sales?
- How does sales performance trend over time?
- Are revenues concentrated in specific products?
- What packaging sizes dominate customer preference?

---

## Key Insights
- Sales peak at 17:00, indicating strong late-afternoon demand.
- Weekend sales show higher per-day intensity compared to weekdays.
- Sales increase significantly in Q3–Q4, with November as the peak month.
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in a small number of core products.
- Demand is dominated by single-serve packaging (Cup & 250ml).

## Business Implications
- Align staffing and inventory with peak demand hours.
- Prioritize core product inventory to reduce stock-out risk.
- Leverage high-performing months for promotional campaigns.
- Focus marketing on single-serve offerings.
